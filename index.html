What we do and why we do it

What is ecolulu?

help others

It's having a talent and putting it to good use.

At ecolulu, we know there are hidden gems out there, people with amazing skills that aren't being put to good use. We want you to succeed in making your dream job your real job.

find help

It's having a need and finding someone to fill it.

Professional help is expensive and often out of reach on a budget. What if there were non-professional but skilled people out there willing to do your job for less? You get a great deal and they get the experience they need to make that job a career!

realize your dream

It's building up your skills and helping realize your dream.

Get connected with people looking for someone like you. Negotiate your own rates, choose what services you offer, and pick the schedule that suits you and them. With each completed job, you'll improve your skills, have real experience, and be better prepared to reach your career goals.

it’s you

It's people with needs meeting people with skills.
It's you.
